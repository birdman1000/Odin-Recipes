Cacio e Pepe

Something I make pretty often, simple dish

Ingredients

  • spagethii pasta
  • pecorina cheese
  • black pepper

Recipe

  1. boil the noodles, make sure to keep the pasta water when finished
  2. pan roast the black pepper in olive oil on the pan
  3. when the peppeer becomes fragrant, fry the pasta in the pepper and oil
  4. fry the pasta a bit, and then put it into a bowl. Now take the extra pasta water and mix it with the pecorino
  5. let the pecorino sauce cool a bit and then mix with the pasta, voila
