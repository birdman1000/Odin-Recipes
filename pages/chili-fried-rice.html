Chili Fried Ric

My goto spicy dish

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Green Onions
  • Chili Oil
  • Carrots
  • Zucchini
  • Ginger
  • Maggi
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Egg

Recipe

  1. wash and boil rice
  2. Fry the green onions hard in sesame oil, then add the chopped carrots and zucchini
  3. Then add the chopped ginger, adding in the egg to fry after
  4. douse all ingredients in the pan in maggi, 5-6 drops
  5. Then add the rice and mix the ingredients up. Then add the soy sauce and vinegar. Then add a few drops of maggi
  6. Put the rice in a bowl and then add the chili oil as a garnish for each dish
